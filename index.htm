Atmiya University(AU), Gujarat was established in the year 2018, under the recognition of University Grants Commission(UGC). The courses offered by the university is affiliated by AICTE and Gujarat Technological University. AU is an extension to Sarvoday Kelavani Samaj, which was set up with a mission to train students into technologically proficient skilled labor.
